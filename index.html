Bay Area Tire Recycling was established in the Bay Area, California, USA since 2008. It started as a small tire hauler for the community, into a tire recycling facility. Now a worldwide waste tire distributor. Tire Recycling is in our blood. If you were looking for waste tire products, you are in the right place. We are working with tire facilities throughout the entire USA and Puerto Rico.
